About us

____

Triber merchanting activities in many ways act as an “enterprise bridge”, bringing real high value from one continent to another, connecting demand and supply in a way that redefines expertise and effectiveness.

We engage the present with more than the traditional way of thinking and welcome the future with an approach centered with new ideas.

SERVICES

the future of Oil is Triber Group

Oil Trading

Triber oil trading activities primarily deals with the wholesale physical supply of a wide spectrum of petroleum products into West Africa.

Shipping

Seaborne transportation in the oil tanker segment is an integral part of our business. We provide chartering solutions to oil majors, independent producers and trading houses, upholding our values of protection of the environment and lives at sea.

Procurement

We advise and provide sourcing solutions that help reduce supply risks and improve supply chain efficiencies in the rail, oil & gas markets in Nigeria.

Commodities

Soft commodities trading is a recent addition to our business. We are encouraged by the well established markets in Cote d'Ivoire, Ghana and the agricultural revolution in Nigeria.
